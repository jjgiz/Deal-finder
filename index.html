About

Independent deal discovery

DealFinder is a simple shopping research tool for finding notable offers in one place. We review deal details such as sale price, list price, stock notes, category, and time remaining so visitors can scan offers before visiting a merchant.

Disclosure

Link Disclosure

DealFinder links to retailer pages so shoppers can check availability and current prices. The price paid by the shopper is set by the retailer and can change after leaving this site.

Privacy

Privacy Policy

DealFinder stores saved deals in your browser using local storage and saves submitted deal-alert email addresses on the server. We do not ask for account details, payment information, or sensitive personal data. We use analytics and basic click logs to understand which deals visitors find useful.

Contact

Contact

For deal corrections, partnership questions, or removal requests, use the contact details provided by the site owner.
