About

Independent deal discovery

DealFinder is a simple shopping research tool for finding notable offers in one place. We review deal details such as sale price, list price, stock notes, category, and time remaining so visitors can scan offers before visiting a merchant.

Disclosure

Affiliate Disclosure

DealFinder may earn a commission when visitors click qualifying merchant links or complete a purchase. This does not change the price paid by the shopper. Deals are selected for relevance, discount strength, and availability.

Privacy

Privacy Policy

DealFinder stores saved deals in your browser using local storage. We do not ask for account details, payment information, or sensitive personal data in this prototype. Server logs may include basic request information used to keep the service working.

Contact

Contact

For deal corrections, partnership questions, or removal requests, use the contact details provided through the publisher account or the merchant network where the offer appears.
